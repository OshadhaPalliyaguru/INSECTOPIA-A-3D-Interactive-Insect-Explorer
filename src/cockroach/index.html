COCKROACH
01
THE COCKROACH

Cockroaches have been around for over 300 million years, surviving mass extinctions and adapting to nearly any environment.

02
SURVIVOR

They can go without food for weeks, endure harsh conditions, and even survive brief decapitation. While often seen as pests, they are vital recyclers of organic matter in nature.

03
SUMMARY

Cockroaches are unmatched in their resilience, capable of withstanding conditions that would kill most other life forms. They are survivors in every sense, thriving through adaptability and durability.
