THE CENTIPEDE
01
The Centepede

Centipedes are fast, agile hunters with many legs, enabling them to chase down and capture prey in dark, damp environments.

02
PREDATOR

Using venomous forcipules, they immobilize insects, spiders, and even small vertebrates. Despite their fearsome look, they help maintain balance in ecosystems by controlling pest populations.

03
SUMMARY

Centipedes are stealthy and efficient predators that strike with speed and precision. Their unique body design and hunting abilities have made them important pest controllers in nature, even if they inspire fear in humans.
