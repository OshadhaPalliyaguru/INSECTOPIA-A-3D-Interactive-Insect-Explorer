The Bee

Bees are among the most important insects for our survival, pollinating countless plants that produce fruits, vegetables, and seeds. Their daily work sustains ecosystems and food supplies worldwide.

02
POLLINATION

Inside a hive, bees live in a highly organized society. Worker bees gather nectar, drones support reproduction, and the queen lays thousands of eggs to maintain the colony. Honey, their natural creation, has been treasured by humans for centuries.
